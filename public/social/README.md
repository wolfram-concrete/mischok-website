# MISCHOK Social And Editorial Trust Material

This folder is a content depot for real trust signals that can be used selectively on the MISCHOK website. It is not a mandate to build a standalone news section.

## Intended Use

Use these assets only when a page section needs more evidence from real public activity, for example:

- editorial proof around expertise
- conference or publication credibility
- case-related visual proof
- links to Kajetan Mischok or MISCHOK social profiles
- small trust cards near topics, references, or CTAs

Do not automatically create a "News" or "Feed" section just because this folder exists.

## Recommended Integration Pattern

Prefer small contextual proof modules over a generic feed:

- A compact "Bekannt aus" strip near expertise/topic sections.
- One or two editorial cards next to "Themen, ueber die wir nachdenken".
- A case proof block near references.
- A small LinkedIn CTA in a person or trust section.

Keep the tone calm and editorial. No fake timestamps, no fake engagement metrics, no fake LinkedIn copy.
